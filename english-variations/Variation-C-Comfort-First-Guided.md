# Variation C: Comfort-First Guided

## Philosophy

This variation prioritizes **student psychological safety** above all else. Inspired by
Yeji's approach for absolute beginners, every page has explicit tutor scripts, bilingual
guidance, and built-in safety nets (translation tools, hints). The tutor is never
uncertain about what to say, and the student is never left feeling lost.

Best for: True absolute beginners, anxious learners, or students with low confidence.

**Key Features:**
- Full tutor scripts on every page (Yeji)
- Bilingual (Korean + English) guidance throughout (Yeji)
- "Mission complete" praise system at wrap-up (Yeji)
- Translation tool as safety net (Yeji)
- 2 patterns per lesson, no grammar explanation — just meaning (Yeji)
- Tutor writes what student says (Yeji)
- Progressive challenge: preview → surprise questions (Yeji)
- 25 minutes total

---

## Lesson Flow (8 Pages)

| Page | Activity                     | Time  | Student Comfort | In Preview? |
|------|------------------------------|-------|-----------------|-------------|
| 1    | Welcome & Warm-Up            | 2 min | ★★★★★           | Yes         |
| 2    | Pattern Intro + Vocab        | 3 min | ★★★★★           | Yes         |
| 3    | Sentence Making              | 4 min | ★★★★☆           | Yes         |
| 4    | Dialogue (Full)              | 3 min | ★★★★★           | Yes         |
| 5    | Dialogue (Blanked)           | 4 min | ★★★☆☆           | Yes         |
| 6    | Tutor Surprise Questions     | 3 min | ★★☆☆☆           | NO          |
| 7    | Ask the Tutor                | 3 min | ★★☆☆☆           | NO          |
| 8    | Wrap-Up & Mission Complete   | 3 min | ★★★★★           | Yes         |
