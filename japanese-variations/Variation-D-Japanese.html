Today's Patterns

PATTERN 1 — Saying what something IS
私wa·ta·shi はwa [学生ga·ku·se·i] ですde·su。
Watashi wa [gakusei] desu. — I am a [student].

Roleplay — First Day at Japanese Class

You just arrived at a Japanese class abroad. Meet another student (the tutor) and introduce yourself!

Rules
✓ Say your name and role using desu
✓ Ask at least 1 question using desu ka?
✓ Say 1 thing that is NOT true using ja arimasen
✗ No answering with just hai or iie — full sentences only
