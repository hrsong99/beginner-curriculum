これkoreはwaなんnanですdesuかka？
これkore・それsore・あれare

What is this? — this / that / that over there

If you have questions, feel free to ask your sensei during the lesson!

Note: Level 4+ classes are conducted 100% in Japanese.

PATTERN DRILL: DEMONSTRATIVES (しじごshijigo)

PATTERN: これkore/それsore/あれareはwa [noun]ですdesu。

「いっしょによみましょう。わたしのあとにリピートしてください。」
"Let's read together. Repeat after me."

1. Read Together:

• これkoreはwaほんhonですdesu。 This is a book.
• それsoreはwaペンpenですdesu。 That is a pen.
• あれareはwaとけいtokeiですdesu。 That over there is a clock.
「ぶんをよんでみてください。くうらんのところもいれて、ぜんぶよんでみましょう！」
"Try reading the sentence. Fill in the blank and read the whole thing!"

2. Fill in the Blanks:

• （　　）はwaほんhonですdesu。 This is a book.
• （　　）はwaペンpenですdesu。 That (near you) is a pen.
• （　　）はwaとけいtokeiですdesu。 That (far away) is a clock.
「えいごをにほんごにしてみてください。ヒントをみてもいいですよ。」
"Try turning the English into Japanese. You can look at the hint."

3. Translate (Translate to Japanese):

• This is a phone.
(this = これkore / phone = でんわdenwa)
• That over there is a school.
(that over there = あれare / school = がっこうgakkō)
「じぶんのぶんをつくってみましょう！なにかをさしてぶんをつくってね。」
"Try making your own sentence! Point at something and make a sentence."

REAL-LIFE PRACTICE: ROLEPLAY

"The Lost & Found"

「これからロールプレイをします。おとしものコーナーにいるつもりで、かいわしましょう！」
"We're going to do a roleplay. Imagine you're at the lost & found, and let's have a conversation!"

SCENARIO:
You are at the school lost & found. The tutor works there. Ask about items and find your things!

GOAL: Find your things!

RULES:
